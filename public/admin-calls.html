CALL CENTER SETUP GUIDE
Follow these steps to get your EVL phone system running
✅
STEP 1 — Call Log & Tracking
EVL Call Center admin page is live. Log calls, track metrics, view history. All stored locally and linked to Mastermind CRM.
✅ COMPLETE — FREE
2
STEP 2 — Get a Dedicated EVL Phone Number
Get a professional business number separate from your personal cell. Two options:

Option A — Google Voice (FREE): Go to voice.google.com → Choose a number with your area code → Forward to your cell. Takes 5 minutes. Best for starting out.

Option B — OpenPhone ($13/month): openphone.com → Full business phone system, text from same number, call recording, team inbox.
⏳ PENDING — FREE or $13/month
🆓 Google Voice — FREE 📞 OpenPhone — $13/mo
3
STEP 3 — Record Your Greeting
Record the EVL greeting script using your phone or computer microphone. Upload to Google Voice or OpenPhone. Use the script in the Directory Tree tab.

Pro tip: Record in a quiet room. Speak slowly and confidently. The greeting is the first impression of your brand.
⏳ PENDING — FREE
4
STEP 4 — Set Up Directory Tree / IVR
Configure the press-1-for-X menu in Google Voice or OpenPhone. Use the EVL Directory Tree from the previous tab.

Google Voice: Settings → Calls → Set up forwarding rules
OpenPhone: Phone Numbers → IVR → Add menu options
⏳ PENDING — FREE
5
STEP 5 — Upgrade to Twilio (When Ready)
When call volume grows, upgrade to Twilio for full integration with Mastermind CRM — automatic call logging, customer ID matching, voicemail transcription, and SMS from the same number.

Cost: ~$15-25/month vs CarWars at $400-800/month. Same features, 95% savings.
⏳ FUTURE — $15-25/month
🔗 twilio.com
6
STEP 6 — Add EVL Number to Website
Once you have your EVL number, add it to the Contact page, footer, and all customer-facing pages. Update admin-comms templates with the real number. Make it click-to-call on mobile.
⏳ PENDING — FREE
