💰 IF YOU HAVE A LOAN — HOW PAYOFF WORKS

Most vehicles submitted to the Bidding Dealer network still have an active loan — that's completely normal and Bidding Dealer partners handle this every day.

  • Once you accept an offer, EVL orders a 20-day payoff quote directly from your lender.
  • Your loan continues to accrue per diem interest (daily interest) until payoff is received — this is standard and already factored into your final numbers.
  • The winning Bidding Dealer partner pays your lender directly — you never handle the payoff yourself.
  • Any difference between the offer amount and your payoff (equity or shortfall) is settled with you directly once the payoff clears.
