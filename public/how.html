Step Two

EVL Searches Every Dealer

We query our live database of 6 million+ vehicles across every franchise and independent dealer in America. We find not just what is available — but who is motivated to deal.

Got Questions

Frequently Asked

How does EVL make money if you charge me nothing?+
EVL earns referral relationships and volume-based dealer partnerships. We never charge customers a fee, and dealers don't pay us more for recommending them — so our only incentive is finding you the best possible deal to earn your referral and repeat business.
Can you really get a better deal than I can negotiate myself?+
Consistently, yes — by a significant margin. We have access to dealer invoice data, holdback figures, regional incentive stacking information, and current lease money factors that dealers never publish. Combined with volume buying power, we routinely save customers $3,000–$15,000 beyond what they could negotiate alone.
Do you handle leases or just purchases?+
Both. For leases, we negotiate money factor (the lease equivalent of APR), residual value, acquisition fees, and any dealer markup on top of those numbers. Most customers have no idea these are negotiable — dealers count on that. Our average lease savings is $80–$220/month.
What if I have a trade-in?+
Use our Trade-In tool to get an instant EVL appraisal based on your VIN. We provide an independent valuation so you know exactly what your vehicle is worth before any negotiation begins. We then factor your trade into the overall deal to maximize total savings.
How long does the whole process take?+
Most customers go from first search to approved deal in 24–48 hours. Delivery typically follows within 48–72 hours of deal approval, depending on vehicle location. Rush delivery is available for an additional logistics fee.
Can you find vehicles that aren't listed online?+
Yes. We have direct dealer relationships across the country and can locate in-transit vehicles, dealer trades, and factory orders not yet in any public database. If the vehicle exists, we can find it.
Is there any obligation when I submit a search?+
Zero obligation until you sign a purchase agreement. You can search, get deals presented, and walk away at any point with no cost and no pressure. We only succeed when you're genuinely happy with your deal.
