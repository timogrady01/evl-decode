NEW VEHICLE LEASE
Standard lease on a new vehicle directly from a manufacturer program. Best for those who want the latest model with full warranty coverage.

  • Latest technology and features
  • Conquest and loyalty incentives available
  • Subvented (special) money factors on select models

USED / OFF-LEASE
Lease a pre-owned or off-lease vehicle. Significantly lower payments than new. Factory maintained, low mileage, often CPO eligible. EVL's best-value recommendation.
  • 100-200$/mo less than new vehicle
  • Factory-maintained condition
  • Under 36,000 miles typical
  • CPO warranty often available
🔄
LEASE TRANSFER
Take over an existing lease from someone who needs to exit early. Short remaining terms, often with seller incentives. Platforms: SwapALease, LeaseTrader, EVL network.
  • Often includes seller cash incentive
  • Short commitment (12-24 mo remaining)
  • Premium vehicles at established payments
  • 2-4 week transfer process typical
🏢
BUSINESS / FLEET
Lease vehicles for business use. Tax advantages, fleet pricing, predictable monthly expenses. EVL advises on fleet structure and connects to fleet-capable dealers.
  • Payments typically tax deductible
  • Fleet pricing available 3+ vehicles
  • Predictable monthly budget
  • Upgrade cycle every 2-3 years

WHAT YOU PAY AT SIGNING

INCEPTION FEES — THE BLINDSIDE

Most customers focus only on the monthly payment — then sit down at the finance desk and discover they owe thousands more at signing. EVL shows you every fee before you walk in.

First Month Payment
REQUIRED
Your monthly payment amount
Always required. Usually rolled into total drive-off cost.
Security Deposit
NEGOTIABLE
$0 — $1,000+
Many brands have eliminated this. Always negotiate to $0. MSDs can lower MF — EVL advises when this makes sense.
Acquisition Fee
REQUIRED
$595 — $1,095
Charged by the captive finance company (TFS, BMWFS, etc.) — NOT negotiable. Can be rolled into cap cost vs. paid upfront.
Doc / Admin Fee
REQUIRED
$150 — $499
Dealer charge. In Texas, capped by law. Negotiable on cap cost offset — EVL factors this in.
Registration & Title
REQUIRED
~$150-250 (Texas)
Must be paid upfront. Cannot be rolled into most leases. Texas is relatively low vs other states.
Sales Tax (Texas)
REQUIRED
6.25% on monthly payment
Texas taxes the monthly payment — not the full vehicle price. This is actually favorable vs buying outright.
Disposition Fee
NEGOTIABLE
$300 — $500 (at lease end)
Charged at turn-in if you don't buy or re-lease. EVL ALWAYS gets this waived in writing at signing. Most dealers agree — they just never volunteer it.
Cap Cost Reduction
YOUR CHOICE
Optional down payment
EVL advises against large cap reductions — you lose that money if the car is totaled. Smaller is smarter on a lease.
⚠️ THE DISPOSITION FEE TRAP
At lease end, if you return the car and don't buy or re-lease with the same brand, dealers charge $300-500. EVL always secures a written waiver at signing. Most salespeople will agree — they just never volunteer it. This one conversation saves you hundreds at turn-in.
LEASE END INTELLIGENCE

EARLY EXIT & WEAR/TEAR — KNOW BEFORE YOU SIGN

EARLY TERMINATION — THE TRAP
💸
You owe remaining payments PLUS fees
Unlike a car loan, you cannot simply return a lease early. You owe remaining payments, early termination fees, and the difference between residual value and current market value.
🔄
Lease transfer is your escape hatch
SwapALease and Lease Trader let you transfer your lease to another qualified buyer. EVL coordinates this process — transfer fee of $300-500 beats thousands in termination penalties.
⚠️
GAP insurance is critical on leases
If the car is totaled, your insurance may not cover the full remaining lease obligation. GAP bridges this gap. EVL always recommends GAP on leases — and finds it cheaper than dealer pricing.
📊
The 12-month breakeven rule
Never trade out of a lease more than 12 months early without running the full financial analysis. EVL calculates the exact breakeven point before you make any decision.
WEAR & TEAR — WHAT ACTUALLY GETS CHARGED
✅ NORMAL — No charge
• Small door dings under 1/2 inch
• Minor scuffs on bumpers
• Light interior wear
• Small chips on windshield edges
🔴 EXCESS — You pay
• Dents larger than 1/2 inch
• Deep scratches through paint
• Torn or burned upholstery
• Missing components/features
• Tires below 2/32 tread depth
⚡ EVL PRO TIPS
• Get wear/tear standards in writing before signing
• Professional detail before turn-in
• PDR (paintless dent repair) at lease end costs $100-200 and saves hundreds
• Document every ding with photos at delivery day
THE MATH THEY DON'T SHOW YOU

MONEY FACTOR → APR CONVERSION

Dealers quote money factors like 0.00225 because it sounds small. Here's how to convert any MF to a real APR — instantly.

The Conversion Formula
MF × 2,400 = APR %
Example: 0.00225 × 2400 = 5.4% APR
MF: 0.001002.4% APR🟢 EXCELLENT
MF: 0.001503.6% APR🟢 STRONG
MF: 0.002004.8% APR🟡 FAIR
MF: 0.002255.4% APR🟡 AVERAGE
MF: 0.002806.7% APR🔴 HIGH
MF: 0.00350+8.4%+ APR🔴 DEALER MARKUP
⚠️ DEALER MF MARKUP: Dealers can mark up the money factor by up to 0.0008 above the buy rate. On a $45,000 vehicle that's approximately $54/month extra — $1,944 over 36 months. EVL identifies and eliminates dealer MF markup before you sign.
