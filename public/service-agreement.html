EVL Vehicle Concierge Service Agreement

This Service Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into between Express Vehicle Locators ("EVL," "Agent") and the Customer identified above ("Customer," "Principal"), effective upon the date of electronic execution below.

1. Scope of Services

EVL agrees to perform the services selected above on behalf of Customer. All services are provided on a flat-fee basis. EVL earns no commission, referral fee, or compensation from any dealer, lender, or buyer.

2. Limited Power of Attorney

Customer hereby appoints EVL as Customer's limited attorney-in-fact, with authority to: (a) contact dealers, wholesalers, lenders, and trade-in buyers on Customer's behalf; (b) negotiate vehicle price, fees, financing rates, lease terms, and trade-in values; (c) receive and present offers to Customer for approval; and (d) disclose Customer's vehicle preferences and budget to relevant parties.

This Limited Power of Attorney is specific to the transaction described herein, is non-durable, and terminates automatically upon: (i) Customer's written revocation; (ii) completion of the transaction; or (iii) 90 days from execution, whichever occurs first.

3. Customer Retains Final Authority

EVL may NOT, under any circumstances: (a) execute any purchase, lease, or financing agreement without Customer's explicit written approval; (b) transfer funds on Customer's behalf; (c) accept delivery of any vehicle without Customer's consent; or (d) make representations about Customer's creditworthiness beyond what Customer has disclosed.

No deal is final until Customer provides explicit written approval.

4. Dealer Confidentiality

EVL will not reveal Customer's identity, contact information, or location to any dealer, lender, or buyer during the search and negotiation phase. Customer's information is disclosed only at Customer's direction upon deal approval.

5. Service Fees

Customer agrees to pay EVL the fees listed above upon execution of this Agreement. If all four services are selected, the EVL Complete Package rate of $795 applies. Fees are earned upon commencement of service and are non-refundable except as provided in EVL's Refund Policy.

6. No Guarantee of Outcome

EVL makes commercially reasonable efforts to fulfill the services requested. EVL does not guarantee any specific vehicle availability, price, rate, trade-in value, or delivery timeline. Market conditions are outside EVL's control.

7. Limitation of Liability

EVL's total liability under this Agreement shall not exceed the total service fees paid by Customer. EVL is not liable for the acts or omissions of any dealer, lender, or buyer.

8. Governing Law

This Agreement is governed by the laws of the State of Texas. Any disputes shall be resolved by binding arbitration in Texas under AAA rules.

9. Electronic Signature

Customer agrees that an electronic signature, whether typed or drawn, constitutes a legally binding signature under the Electronic Signatures in Global and National Commerce Act (ESIGN) and the Uniform Electronic Transactions Act (UETA), with the same force and effect as a handwritten signature.

10. Entire Agreement

This Agreement, together with EVL's Terms of Service and Privacy Policy, constitutes the entire agreement between the parties and supersedes all prior discussions, negotiations, and understandings.
