Frequently Asked Questions
What documents should I upload to my vault? +
Your Monroney/window sticker, buyer's order, all warranty booklets, GAP insurance documents, tire and wheel protection, paint protection, We-Owe forms, service records, and any modification receipts. EVL will guide you through uploading everything at activation.
How does the trade-in timing analytics work? +
EVL monitors your vehicle's depreciation curve against current market conditions for your specific year, make, model, mileage, and region. We alert you when the data shows your vehicle is at peak resale value — before it depreciates further. This is the same intelligence automotive professionals use internally.
Can you really find better service prices than my dealer? +
Yes — and we will even send you to a competitor dealership or independent shop if the price is better. EVL's loyalty is to the customer, not the dealer. We find current coupons, service specials, and compare prices across all providers in your area for every service your vehicle needs.
What if I already have my documents scattered everywhere? +
That is exactly why the vault exists. Your EVL advisor will walk you through a document recovery process at activation — identifying what you have, what you are missing, and how to obtain copies of missing documents from the dealership or DMV.
Is there a contract or cancellation fee? +
No contract, no cancellation fee. You can cancel anytime within the first 30 days for a full refund. After 30 days your annual subscription runs its course and simply does not renew unless you choose to continue.
What is included in the Elite EVL advisor access? +
Elite members get direct access to a seasoned automotive professional — the same people who work inside high-volume dealerships every day. They review your specific situation: your deal, your trade, your service history, and your options — and give you straight answers with no agenda other than your best interest.
